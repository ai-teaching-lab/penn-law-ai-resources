DO NOT USE THE FREE VERSION.
Free tiers run older, weaker models — what you experience is not what AI actually is. They are slower, rate-limited, and will use your data to train future models. Claude Pro and ChatGPT Plus are $20/month. If you are doing any real work and you are not paying for it, you are doing it wrong.
02 From Talking to Doing

The first wave was conversational — ask, receive, copy-paste. That era is over. We're now in the second, more consequential shift: AI as actor.

Then
Chatbot
  • You prompt, it responds
  • Human copies output manually
  • One turn at a time
  • Passive tool
Now
Agent
  • Browses the web autonomously
  • Writes and runs code
  • Manages files and email
  • Chains multi-step tasks

Claude Computer Use, ChatGPT Operator, Gemini with Workspace — these tools don't just answer, they act. Already showing up in contract review, due diligence, and research workflows at major firms.

Students entering practice in 2–3 years will be expected to supervise AI agents, not just prompt chatbots. That is a different skill — and a different professional responsibility question.

The frame that works: think of AI as a junior colleague who needs supervision, not a search engine that needs better queries.
